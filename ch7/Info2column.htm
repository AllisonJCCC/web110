Fun things to do in Kansas City in the spring.

1. National WWI Museum and Memorial

The two original buildings above ground house visiting exhibits in two outstanding halls.

2. Kauffman Stadium

Despite being built in 1973 Kauffman is currently the sixth oldest Major League Baseball stadium.

3. Kansas City Zoo

From polar bears and penguins to Kangaroos, it covers all the wildlife regions of the world.
